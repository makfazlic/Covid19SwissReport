Report visually presents publically known Swiss COVID-19 data and is updated after a complete day, unlike the Official Swiss Government website where you can find more accurate and more frequently published reports (link in the navigation bar)
Data used belongs to John Hopkins University (Baltimore, USA). The data is dynamically pulled from the source and processed to be analysed and presented. Resources can be found on their GitHub page: Click Here
The ambition for the creation of this page was to make the information regarding the COVID-19 pandemic easier to interpret and follow by the wider public. The creator does not take any responsibility for the accuracy of the data presented.
